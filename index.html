How It Works

Simple as 1-2-3

Poowangi creates an aromatic barrier on the water's surface, trapping odor beneath before it escapes.

1

Spray

Spray 3-5 spritz of Poowangi into the toilet bowl water before you sit down. The essential oils form a protective layer on the water's surface.

2

Go

Do your business as usual. The aromatic oil barrier traps all odors beneath the water surface, so nothing escapes into the air.

3

Flush

Flush and walk away with confidence. The bathroom smells fresh and pleasant — no one will ever know what happened.
